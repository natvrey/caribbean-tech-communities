| Ekosistèm Fintèk Ayiti | Website, LinkedIn | Where Haitian FinTech Builds, Grows, and Leads.

Ekosistèm Fintek Ayiti is the beating heart of Haiti’s FinTech movement—a powerful network of professionals, startups, investors, entrepreneurs, and policymakers reshaping the future of finance in the Caribbean.

We exist to:
•	Accelerate FinTech adoption across the region
•	Shine a spotlight on bold, Haitian-led innovation
•	Advocate for smart, inclusive, and sustainable policies
•	Empower our members with mentorship, education, and real support.

Whether you're here to connect, grow, or discover FinTech built by Haitians—this is where it all starts.


Kote Fintèk Ayiti Ap Bati, Devlope, epi Dirije.

Ekosistèm Fintèk Ayiti se kè mouvman Fintèk Ayisyen an — yon rezo solid pwofesyonèl, startup, antreprenè, envestisè, ak desizyonè k ap reimajine avni finans lan nan Karayib la.

Nou la pou:
	•	Akselere adopsyon Fintèk atravè rejyon an
	•	Mete limyè sou inovasyon brav ak ayisyen
	•	Defann politik ki dirab, enkliziv, e entèlijan
	•	Bay manm nou yo zouti pou grandi: mentor, fòmasyon, ak sipò solid.

# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

This project aims to maintain a useful, public directory of Caribbean tech communities. Participation should remain respectful, factual, and focused on the quality of the directory.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Submit accurate information
- Respect privacy and community norms
- Avoid promotional abuse and spam
- Assume good faith, but verify contributions carefully

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Submitting misleading or malicious links
- Publishing private invite links without permission
- Repeated spam submissions
- Discriminatory or abusive conduct

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, reject, or remove submissions that do not meet the standards of this repository. Repeated abuse may result in blocked participation.
